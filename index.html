Interactive Caregiving

At Comfort Keepers of Greensboro, we practice Interactive Caregiving—a holistic approach that engages the whole person: mind, body, and spirit. Rather than simply performing tasks for seniors, our caregivers actively involve them in their own care.

This approach fosters independence, a sense of purpose, and allows seniors to continue contributing to everyday activities. When seniors stay engaged with the things that matter most—accomplishing daily tasks, connecting with family and friends—they enjoy a significantly higher quality of life.
